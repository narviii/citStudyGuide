# International engagement

### [G8](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Group_of_Eight)
Group of leading industrialized countries, including Canada, the United States, Germany, the United Kingdom, Italy, France, Japan and Russia.
### [NAFTA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_American_Free_Trade_Agreement)
North American Free Trade Agreement. Enacts free trade between Canada and the United States since 1988. Mexico became a partner in 1994.
### [NATO](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NATO)
North Atlantic Treaty Organization, a military alliance of democratic countries of the West.
### [NORAD](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_American_Aerospace_Defense_Command)
North American Aerospace Defence Command, an alliance with the United States.
